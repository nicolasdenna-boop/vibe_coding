## 7. Headhunter note on compensation

€120k+ is a sensible **floor**, but be aware it likely **under-prices you**. An
ex-Hermès Commercial Director with Executive Committee experience, targeting
CCO/GM roles in European luxury, typically commands materially more at base
(plus bonus/LTI) depending on company size. Set €120k as the floor that won't
cap you — but in conversations, anchor on **scope and seniority first**, and let
the package follow the level. Don't volunteer a number early.
